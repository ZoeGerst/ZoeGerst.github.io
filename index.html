Welcome to my personal website! I hold a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science from Oregon State University, where my passion for technology and creativity merged seamlessly. With a keen interest in video game development, computer animation, 2D animation, and computer graphics, I am driven by the endless possibilities technology offers in shaping immersive digital experiences. Proficient in languages such as C, C++, Python, Rust, HTML, and OpenGL, I strive to push the boundaries of innovation in my projects. Beyond the realm of coding, I am adept in Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, and Animate, leveraging these tools to bring my creative visions to life.

Outside of the tech sphere, I hold significant achievements including a Second Degree Black Belt in Tae Kwon Do and Hop Kido. My favorite TV show is Gravity Falls. One of my favorite hobbies include baking. I love hanging out with my friends in my spare time. Passionate, versatile, and driven, I am committed to exploring new horizons in the ever-evolving landscape of technology and design.
