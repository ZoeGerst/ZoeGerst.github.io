Demon Dash

Demon Dash is a concept for a TV show I came up with about a human girl named Abi who gets a job as a food delivery driver in Hell.

Abi (right) is the main character of Demon Dash. She delivers food throughout Hell with her friend and coworker Earl (left). Abi is always eager to help when she can and is a people pleaser while Earl is lazy and always looks for shortcuts.

Behemoth is the restaurant owner and head chef. He worked as Satan's personal chef for many years before he opened his own place. Rough and tough personality. Easily angered. Eventually becomes a mentor figure for Abi.

The Ring of Envy, concept art of what deliverying food in Hell would look like. In this portrait, Abi and Earl are deliverying tacos to Leviathan.

A rough demo for the opening sequence of Demon Dash.
